Frequently Asked Questions

Is it possible to get direct admission to government engineering colleges?

It is impossible to get direct admission to a government engineering college.

Is it possible to get admission to a government engineering college without JEE Main?

Yes! It is possible to get admitted to government engineering colleges through state-level counselling.

Is it necessary to get 75% above in 12th for getting go college?

No , You can also get admission in government colleges below this percentile

How to get direct admission to a top private engineering college in India?

As per the guideline of MHRD. There are 15% reserved seats. Under management quota in top Pvt. engineering colleges expect all campuses of BITS, Thapar University (Patiala), VIT Vellore/Chennai, Shiv Nadar University, LNMIT Jaipur.

What is JoSAA Counselling?

Joint Seat Allocation Authority is known as JoSAA, is a counseling agency established by MHRD and regulates the admission to 114 institutions administrated by Govt. of India.

What is ComedK?

Karnataka Professional Colleges Foundation, in their endeavour to offer an effective, fair and objective testing procedure to determine merit of students seeking admission to the member institutions, have formed “Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka” (COMEDK).
